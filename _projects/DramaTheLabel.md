# Drama The Label
## Small business, boutique clothing
## Contracting optimisation engineer, direct development with CEO 

### Problem
Sales team were spending 2 weeks, 4 times per year (320 hours), collating and manually entering orders from customers to send through to suppliers. 

### Objectives & Constraints
- Reducing data entry time from 320 hours to maximum 160 hours
- Simple system that doesn't require heavy tech knowledge, easily self-customisable across the year

### Discovery
- Bottleneck in receiving excel sheet with orders and manually adding up orders across 100+ customers for 500+ items, to send out to several suppliers. 

### Solution Overview
Fully automated desktop app that takes a folder of the customer orders and collates all orders into a final spread sheet to send out to suppliers. The header sheet is editable for updates to clothing options. 

### Implementation Highlights
- Python backend
- Tkinter frontend 
- Windows application

### Outcomes
- Reduced time from 320 hours per year, to 1.5 hours per year (99.5% decrease in time)
- A simple application with clear, intuitive work flow and high customisability
- This was a fantastic project to exercise my understanding of business needs
